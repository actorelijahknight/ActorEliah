Cookies & analytics

By default, no cookies or analytics scripts run on this website. On first visit, a cookie banner asks for consent to use Microsoft Clarity, an analytics tool that helps understand how the site is used so it can be improved. No Clarity script or cookie loads until "Accept" is selected in the banner. Choosing "Reject", or leaving the banner unanswered, keeps Clarity switched off entirely. This choice can be changed at any time via "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page.

When consent is given, Microsoft Clarity (operated by Microsoft) collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, clicks, scroll behavior, and device or browser information, and may process this data as described in the Microsoft Privacy Statement. The legal basis for this processing is consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR / Swiss FADP). Consent can be withdrawn at any time via "Cookie Settings" in the footer; once withdrawn, no further data is sent to Microsoft Clarity from that browser.

If you choose to play a video, the embedded YouTube player loads and your IP address and other technical data are transmitted to Google, governed by YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.
